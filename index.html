About me

Hi! My name is Rachel Wang (王若麟). I am a software engineer and I am interested in developing explainable AI models that integrate multimodal data— such as electronic health records (EHR) and clinical notes. Additionally, I am focused on exploring human-AI interaction strategies that promote effective collaboration between AI-driven diagnostic tools and human-users.

I received my MSc in Medical Engineering (Image and Data Processing) from University of Erlangen-Nuremberg, where I worked with Jithin Yohannan and Chris Bradley on Glaucoma prediction, Mathias Unberath and Katharina Breininger on human-AI interaction. I received my BSc in Electrical Engineering from North China Electric Power University.
